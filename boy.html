Scout statue

    The Boy Scouts of the Philippines (BSP) in collaboration with the City Government on Wednesday afternoon,

    November 15, spearheaded the groundbreaking ceremony for the construction of the centennial monument in

    the area.Those who graced the ceremony were: engineer Rogelio Villa, BSP secretary general; artist

    Nemesio Miranda; youth mayor KC Madrazo; lawyer Emilio Aquino, Securities and Exchange Commission

    (SEC) commissioner; BSP regional chair Alrasheed Sakalahul; Councilor Rommel Agan; and Zamboanga

    Economic Zone chairperson and administrator Christopher Lawrence Arnuco.
