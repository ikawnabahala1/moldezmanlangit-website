Curacha

    It is a regional specialty of the Zamboanga City. The sauce was invented by Maria Teresa Camins Alavar


    and is originally served in the Alavar Seafood Restaurant. The restaurant now sells the original Alavar


    sauce recipe in packets. It is a variant of the traditional ginataang curacha (curacha in coconut milk).


    The recipe can also be made with mud crabs (cangrejo) or prawns (locon).
